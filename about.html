About Aisha University

Aisha University of Nigeria is located in Abuja, Nigeria. It is a private university which was established in 2022 to provide quality tertiary education for the youth who graduated high-school seeeking to further their education and pursue their dream careers. Aisha University prides itself over its high population of about 800 students, and offers 5 undergraduate programs and 0 postgraduate programs. Aisha University of Nigeria is a proud member of the Chosen United Tertiary Education Network, the third largest pan-African Private Higher Education Network present in 5 African countries

During the course of time, Aisha University has participated in some activities and sports which are mentioned below:

  • Aisha University has participated in the 2022 World Olympics Tournament
  • It has also engaged in National Presidential Career's Day along with Baze University
  • It Contributed to The World Animal's Research Day
